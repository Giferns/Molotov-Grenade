[en]
CVAR_ACCESS = Access flags - to purchase or issue molotovs (requires "abc" / empty string "" - purchase available to all)
CVAR_EQUIP_ACCESS = How many grenades to give out at spawn.
CVAR_CHECK_BUYZONE = Check if the player is in the buy zone.
CVAR_COST = Molotov price
CVAR_BUY_LIMIT = How many molotov can be bought in one round (value: -1 removes the limit)
CVAR_LIMIT_ROUND = How many molotov will be available per round after the game starts.
CVAR_HIT_PLAYER = How much damage to deal when the molotov hits the players body.
CVAR_KILLFEED = Whether to show the prefix [ᴍᴏʟᴏᴛᴏᴠ] next to the name in killfeed.
CVAR_RADIUS = Molotov burning radius.
CVAR_THROWTIME = How many seconds the molotov can stay in flight before exploding
CVAR_DURATION = How many seconds the molotov will burn.
CVAR_DEMAGE_MODE = Who takes damage from the molotov cocktail (0 - opponents only, 1 - opponents and the player who threw the molotov cocktail, 2 - all players)
CVAR_DEMAGE_RADIUS_MODE = How fire damage will be dealt (1 - via Ham_TakeDamage [damage is fixed at any point in the burning radius], 2 - via rg_dmg_radius [damage depends on the range to the burning epicenter and the presence of armor]).
CVAR_DEMAGE_TIME = Damage periodicity
CVAR_DEMAGE_VALUE = Amount of damage done per period (molotov_demage_time)
CVAR_EFFECT_NUM = Number of fire waves that can be drawn
CVAR_SMOKE_TOUCH = Whether to extinguish a molotov cocktail with a smoke grenade.
CVAR_SMOKE_OWNER = Path to the smoke grenade model. (Standard: models/w_smokegrenade.mdl)
CVAR_WATER_TOUCH = Whether to extinguish a molotov cocktail on contact with water
CVAR_SKY_FORCE = Remove a molotov cocktail when it comes in contact with a map skybox
